What Will Be Covered

The purpose of this Just In Time Diesel Outage Workshop is to provide an environment for exploring current event to eminent issues related to a potential diesel shortages and/or unavailability. The workshop is NOT a disaster preparedness exercise but a facilitated discussion to identify key topics and challenges, develop and prioritize mitigation options and solutions, and foster information sharing and problem-solving among participants.

Attendance

This workshop is time sensitive and therefore has a short timespan to fit it into what are sure to be already full calendars. Participants are encouraged to attend whatever portion of the workshop they can. Plenty of handouts will be provided and all participants will receive the resulting Shelby Ready Diesel Outage Playbook.
