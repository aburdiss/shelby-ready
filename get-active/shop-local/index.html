Shop Local

When you shop local, you're making a personal investment in your neighborhood and community. In fact, roughly 70% of money spent at local businesses returns to local activity. Money is kept in the community because locally-owned businesses often purchase from other local businesses, service providers, and farms.

Don't buy from Amazon! Amazon wants to destroy small businesses. Buy from local stores, since you are supporting your neighbor, and not mega corporations who hate how you live.
