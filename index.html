Are you ready?

Shelby Ready is a new organization reclaiming Shelby County's long history of Civil Defense and Citizen Preparedness that will be active in promoting and strengthening Community in Shelby County, Ohio. Shelby Ready is comprised of local responders, stakeholders, officials, and volunteers from across the county that will take an active and integrated role in coordinating disaster and preparedness training and volunteer opportunities for churches, groups, businesses, and individuals. The goal is to help people, families, and neighborhoods to prepare for, respond to, and recover from disasters of all sizes and impacts.

2022 Catastrophic Readiness Planning Event

A Catastrophic Readiness Planning event to explore current events or eminent issues related to a potential diesel shortages and/or unavailability. The workshop is NOT a disaster preparedness exercise, but a facilitated discussion to identify key topics and challenges, develop and prioritize mitigation options and solutions, and foster information sharing and problem-solving among participants.
